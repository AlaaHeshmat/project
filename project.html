Public Policy Information Systems (PPIS)

         Description and Objectives of Public Policy Information Systems (PPIS)
         The faculty of Commerce at Assiut University in Egypt has designed the 
         Public Policy Information Systems (PPIS) program , is the first specialized 
         and unique one of its kind in Africa and the Middle East. This unique program 
         combines the disciplines of public policy with information systems. The courses
         are taught by computer science and public policy faculty members and experts 
        from the digital sector and information systems.
